Our Story

Meet Michael
& Sandee

Est. 2017  |  Weatherford, TX
New Location Coming Soon
"What started as a side job became our calling. We built Outdoor Grill Sales on one belief: take care of the customer first, every single time."

Michael and Sandee Densmore opened Outdoor Grill Sales in Weatherford, TX in 2017. What began as a side hustle fueled by a passion for great barbecue grew into a full-time family business. Today they carry premium grill brands, offer expert repair services, and handle full outdoor kitchen installations.

When you buy a grill from Outdoor Grill Sales, you are not just buying a product. You are getting Michael and Sandee in your corner. Most issues get handled right in the store or over the phone.
