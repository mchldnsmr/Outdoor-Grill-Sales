The Densmore Family

From Side Job
to Full Time Passion

In a small town in Weatherford, Texas, Michael and Sandee Densmore started Outdoor Grill Sales as something to do on the side. Michael had always loved grilling, loved the community that forms around a great backyard cookout, and loved helping people find the right equipment to make it happen.

What started as a side job grew into something neither of them expected. Customers kept coming back. Word spread through Parker County. People started referring their neighbors, their friends, their coworkers. Not because of flashy advertising, but because Michael picked up the phone, answered their questions honestly, and actually fixed what was broken.

Today, Outdoor Grill Sales is a full-time husband and wife operation. Sandee keeps everything running, and Michael is the one who will walk you through every grill on the floor, diagnose your repair over the phone, or drive out to install your new outdoor kitchen. The business grew because the customers told it to.
